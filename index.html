Easy Sunny Coloring - Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

1. General Information

Easy Sunny Coloring is designed to provide a safe and fun coloring experience, especially for children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information.

2. Data Collected

  • Personal Data: The app does not collect personal information such as name, email, or address.
  • Device Data: The app does not collect device identifiers, location, or any sensitive data.
  • Third-Party Services: The app may use technical services (such as Supabase) for anonymous content storage. These services do not collect personal information.

3. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for children under the age of 13 and complies with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-Kids regulations. No personal information is collected from children.

4. Use of Data

  • To improve the coloring experience
  • To detect and fix technical issues
  • To add new features and enhancements

5. Ads and Purchases

The free version of the app may show only child-appropriate ads in compliance with the Google Play Families Policy. No personal information is required for using the app or making purchases.

6. Security

We take reasonable industry-standard measures to protect user data and ensure a safe environment for children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. All updates will be published on this page with the revised date.
