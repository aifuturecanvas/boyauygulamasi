Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous User ID: We create an anonymous identifier to manage your session and purchases. This ID is not linked to your personal information.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, transaction data is processed by RevenueCat and Google Play.
  • AI Feature Usage: If you use the AI coloring feature, your text prompts are sent to our servers to generate images.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or other personal information
  • Location data
  • Photos or files from your device (except what you save in the app)

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Backend services and anonymous authentication
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management
  • Google Play: App distribution and billing

Children's Privacy

Our app is COPPA compliant. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Storage

Your coloring work is stored locally on your device. Anonymous session data is stored on secure servers.

Account & Data Deletion

Easy Sunny Coloring uses anonymous accounts - no registration required.

To Delete Your Data

  • Local Data: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Server Data: Contact us at sunyakids@gmail.com to request deletion of your anonymous session data
  • Purchase History: Managed by Google Play - contact Google support

Contact: sunyakids@gmail.com

Data deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.
